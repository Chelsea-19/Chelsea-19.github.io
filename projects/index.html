Bio · Foundation Models · NTU Research

Evo 2 Genomic Language Model

Designed a multi-scale interpretation framework for Acinetobacter baumannii, spanning replicon-level, fixed-window, dynamic-window, and embedding-based analyses to study species-level genomic regularities and anomalous regions.

  • Built analysis pipelines for long-sequence inference, gene-centric perplexity, and representation extraction.
  • Connected exploratory analysis to a broader genomic agent direction with retrieval and distillation ideas.
  • Positioned the work as an interpretable AI4Science study rather than a benchmark-only exercise.
Why it matters: demonstrates how foundation-model signals can support biological interpretation, not only prediction.

Healthcare · Medical Education · Agent Systems

SimuPatient

An LLM-powered digital patient simulation platform for medical education, helping medical students practice clinical communication and diagnostic reasoning through AI-patient interviews.

  • Built an AI patient generation module for creating diverse, case-specific simulated patients.
  • Designed a dialogue module for realistic medical consultation simulation between users and AI patients.
  • Implemented an OSCE-style assessment module to provide structured performance feedback after each session.
Why it matters: bridges classroom learning and practical interviewing skills with repeatable, low-cost simulation training.

Public Health · Evidence Agent · Drug Repurposing

OrphanCure-AI

A multi-agent system that helps researchers evaluate whether existing drugs may be repurposed for target diseases through automated hypothesis evaluation and evidence mining for specific drug-disease pairs.

  • Uses OpenTargets entity resolution to identify overlaps between drug targets and disease-associated targets.
  • Performs PubMed retrieval and ranking to prioritize high-value supporting literature.
  • Automatically judges and categorizes evidence to produce a structured, auditable assessment workflow.
Why it matters: accelerates early-stage repurposing research with transparent evidence chains and reduced manual screening effort.

Chronic Care · China Healthcare · Clinical Workflow

CarePilot CN

An intelligent chronic disease management platform for patients in mainland China with type 2 diabetes and hypertension, designed to support long-term care and clinician collaboration.

  • Provides patient-facing health consultation and diet risk analysis modules.
  • Supports medication management, reminders, and automated clinical workflow execution.
  • Generates medical summaries and exportable reports for continuous follow-up and care coordination.
Why it matters: demonstrates localized, real-world chronic care management with integrated patient support and clinical operations.
