About Me

I am an MSc student in Biomedical Data Science (AI) at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. My work sits at the intersection of AI for genomics, healthcare decision workflows, and public-health analysis.

I am especially interested in problems where model performance alone is not enough — where the system also needs interpretable structure, traceable evidence, and a clear link back to the underlying scientific or clinical question.

I build AI4Science research systems that connect biological questions, healthcare workflows, and public-health evidence with interpretable modeling, verifiable outputs, and research-to-product thinking. My portfolio is designed for both academic collaborators and hiring teams — each section emphasizes not only what I worked on, but also the scientific question, system design, and available evidence.
